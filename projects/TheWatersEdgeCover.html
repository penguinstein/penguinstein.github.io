About Me

I am a freelance illustrator in the Northwest Arkansas area and graduated from the Kansas City Art Institute with a major in animation. It was there that I discovered my passion for digital painting and illustration. I have since created book covers, shirt designs, and more for projects from Shirtpunch, Modern Druid Incx., Milan Kazarka, the Bella Vista Animal Shelter, and others.
